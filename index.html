Imagine Dragons

Who are Imagine Dragons?

Imagine Dragons are an American Pop Group from Nevada. They first Gained exposure by Dropping their album "Its Time" which put them on the radar for many people. Their Next Releases, "Demons" and "RadioActive" boosted them even further up on the chart, as both albums themselves were very highly ranked. they have won multiple American Music Awards, Billboard Music Awards, a Grammy, and a World Music Award. Their latest Album is Evolve.
